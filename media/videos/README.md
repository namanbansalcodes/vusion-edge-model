# Demo Videos Directory

Place your shelf camera demo videos here.

## Auto-Discovery

The app automatically discovers **ALL videos** in this folder - no specific filename required!

## Supported Formats

- MP4 (recommended, best browser compatibility)
- AVI
- MOV
- WEBM
- MKV

## Recommended Specs

- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 or 1280x720
- **Duration:** 10-60 seconds (videos auto-loop)
- **Frame rate:** 24-30 fps
- **File size:** < 50MB per video

## How to Add Videos

```bash
# Copy all your videos (any filename!)
cp /path/to/your/videos/*.mp4 media/videos/

# Or copy specific videos
cp ~/Downloads/shelf_video_1.mp4 media/videos/
cp ~/Downloads/shelf_video_2.mp4 media/videos/
```

## What Makes a Good Demo Video

Your videos should show:
- ✅ Retail shelves with products
- ✅ Clear view of stock levels
- ✅ Some areas with stock-outs (empty spaces)
- ✅ Good lighting
- ✅ Stable camera (not too shaky)

## Navigation

Once videos are added:
- Use **◀ ▶ buttons** to switch between videos
- Use **arrow keys** (left/right) for keyboard navigation
- Videos **auto-loop** for continuous playback
- See **video counter** (e.g., "2 / 5") at bottom of player

## Sample Sources

If you don't have videos yet:
1. Record a shelf at your local grocery store (with permission!)
